The 1s and 0s of a Quantum Computer
This project will be about quantum computers. A quantum computer is a device that functions differently from reading ones and zeroes. Instead of reading ones and zeroes, they look at the possible outcomes for both.
Quantum computers are not very cost-effective, so the average human can't own a quantum computer (yet).
How Much Does a Quantum Computer Cost to Build?
The average quantum computer can cost from tens of millions to even billions of dollars as some of the parts such as qubits and cryogenic coolers cost up to millions depending on the quality.
