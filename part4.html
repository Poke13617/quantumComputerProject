What are some companies that use quantum computers.
There are a lot of companies that use quantum computers. One of these example's is google's "willow", a quantum computer that could have rendered all personal information useless. The reason for this is that they tried to run the computer, but it gave a lot of personal information along with other special information that they know they shouldn't be able to run. They ended up having to shut this project down in fear that it could completely ruin the internet.
Another company that uses these devices for business and other uses is amazon. They have a quantum computer mainly for research. They are using it to try to create new chips for things like computers and phones. This would be a big breakthrough, and they likely want to be the first to be able to do it.
There is a giant race between these companies to be the first to discover more information on quantum computers. The quantum computer is one of the strongest devices one could have at their disposal, and if that gets in the right hands, it could make a difference to people. The company that helps get the quantum computer to the hands of the public will also likely be favored when people are talking about computer companies.
Another thing that companies could do with the quantum computers would be using it to increase business. It likely would not be completely impossible to make a quantum chip and use it for a server. This would drastically increase website statistics.
